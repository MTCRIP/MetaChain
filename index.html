Having been part of the blockchain space for more than 3.5 years, we have seen enthusiastic booms, depressing busts, sneaky scams, crafty hacks, and in the midst of all this, some of the most fascinating experiments in decentralization, done at the frontier of technology, economics and politics.  

Gradually, we have gained invaluable insights into what it takes to successfully bootstrap a project from an idea, to a global scale.

Building on this, we've created Metachain Capital, a digital asset investment fund focused on supporting some of the most ambitious teams and ingenious technologies in the blockchain space.
